About Me

I am a web developer living in San Diego, California. I earned my Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology from UC San Diego and my background experience includes Marketing and Health & Wellness. I found a passion for coding quite by accident while pursuing another course of study. Shortly after this discovery, I enrolled in a four-month intensive full stack web development program at LEARN Academy. I love coding because it allows me to use both creativity and logic, as well as forcing me to look at challenges from multiple perspectives. Problem-solving drives me and I enjoy breaking things down to their smallest steps. Plus, coding is fun!


Born in Boston, I grew up in Phoenix and attended high school in the Los Angeles Area. Aside from a three-year stint in San Francisco after college, I have lived in the beautiful city of San Diego. My biggest passions outside of coding are music, sports, and animals. You can often find me dancing at a live show, running along the San Diego harbor, rooting for my favorite sports teams (Go Niners!), or at the local dog park.
